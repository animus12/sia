Amazing Facts about Hinatuan Enchanted River

The Hinatuan Enchanted River, also called the Hinatuan Sacred River, is a deep spring river on the island of Mindanao in the Philippines. It flows into the Philippine Sea and the Pacific Ocean at Barangay Talisay, Hinatuan, Surigao del Sur.

It is found between the boundaries of Barangays of Talisay and Cambatong. It earned the moniker “Enchanted River” from the Diplomat Modesto Farolan who described the river in his poem entitled “Rio Encantado”.Let’s take a look at some of the most amazing facts about it;

The river is spit from the cave underneath it is clean and free of any dirt or silt which would usually cloud such a stream. In fact, no one is sure exactly where the saltwater emanates from, although the prevailing theory involves an underground cave system that spits the river out.
